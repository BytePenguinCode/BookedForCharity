Reading Tips: Help your child build a love for reading

Read together daily

Even 10 minutes a day makes a difference.

Let them choose

Support their interests — whether it’s fantasy, animals, or comics.

Create a cozy reading spot

Make reading time feel special and inviting.

Ask questions

Talk about the story to build comprehension and curiosity.

Be a reading role model

Let them see you reading too!

Make Thursdays your book day!

Check out our TYRC posts where we feature a book, share a short summary, and suggest questions to discuss together. Turn it into a weekly reading tradition with your child!

Recommended Tools:

Help support your child’s reading journey with these helpful resources:

  • Epic! — A digital library for kids 12 and under with thousands of books, audiobooks, and educational videos.
  • Libby — A free app that lets you borrow eBooks and audiobooks from your local library.
  • Storyline Online — Well-known actors read children’s books aloud with engaging visuals.
  • PBS Kids Reading Games — Fun, educational games that build reading and vocabulary skills.
  • Book Creator — A simple tool for kids to create their own digital books and stories.

And don’t forget to follow our Thursday Young Readers Circle (TYRC) for weekly book picks and summaries you can read together!
